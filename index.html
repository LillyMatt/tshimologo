Tshimologo Care Centre

Tshimologo Care Centre is a drop-in centre for the homeless people that aims to restore the dignity of individuals ,groups and communnities through promotion of social inclusio, reintegration and holistic service delivery.

Explore Our Services

Services We Provide

Tshimologo Care Centre provides a comprehensive range of services aimed at addressing the diverse needs of homeless individuals. Our dedicated team begins by identifying hot spots within the community, strategically reaching out to areas where homelessness is prevalent. Through our proactive outreach programs, we engage with individuals, offering basic screening and assessments to better understand their specific needs. Our facilities include laundry services, acknowledging the importance of personal hygiene and providing a dignified means to address this essential aspect of well-being. In addition to these practical services, Tshimologo Care Centre offers crucial psychosocial support services, recognizing the mental and emotional challenges faced by the homeless. Our team includes skilled counselors and social workers who provide personalized counseling and ongoing support, guiding individuals towards reintegration, restoring their dignity, and promoting social inclusion within the community.

  • Identification of Hot spots

    The Identification of Hot Spots is a proactive initiative by Tshimologo Care Centre to locate and engage with areas within the community where homelessness is prevalent. By strategically identifying these hot spots, our team can effectively target outreach efforts and provide timely support to those in need. This method enables us to reach individuals experiencing homelessness, assess their immediate requirements, and offer a range of services designed to address their unique challenges.

  • Laundry Facilities

    Tshimologo Care Centre is pleased to announce that we offer Laundry Services on Tuesdays and Wednesdays between 08h00 and 12h00. Recognizing the importance of personal hygiene and clean clothing for the well-being and dignity of individuals facing homelessness, our laundry facilities are dedicated to providing a reliable and accessible service. Clients are welcome to utilize our facilities during the specified hours, ensuring they have access to clean clothes and supporting their overall health and comfort.

  • Nutritional Support

    Tshimologo Care Centre is proud to offer Nutritional Support through our breakfast and lunch programs. Our breakfast service operates from 08:30 to 09:00, providing a nourishing start to the day for individuals facing homelessness. Additionally, our lunch program is available from 12:00 to 13:00, ensuring that those in need have access to a wholesome and fulfilling meal during the midday hours. Recognizing the vital role nutrition plays in overall well-being.We is dedicated to providing regular and nutritious meals, promoting the health of the individuals we serve.

  • Psychosocial Support Services

    Tshimologo Care Centre understands the importance of addressing the mental and emotional well-being of individuals facing homelessness. Our Psychosocial Support Services are designed to provide compassionate assistance to those dealing with the complex challenges associated with homelessness. Our dedicated team of counselors and support staff offer personalized guidance, counseling, and a safe space for individuals to express their thoughts and feelings. By fostering a supportive environment, we aim to help individuals build resilience, cope with stressors, and work towards personal growth and reintegration.

  • Department of Home Affairs ID Online Booking

    Understanding the challenges faced by individuals experiencing homelessness, we are committed to facilitating access to essential documentation. Through this service, clients can conveniently schedule appointments for ID-related services, such as applications or renewals, using our on-site facilities. By providing access to the Department of Home Affairs ID Online Booking, Tshimologo aims to empower individuals to secure crucial identification documents, fostering a pathway towards increased opportunities, social inclusion, and a sense of belonging within the community.

  • Family Reunification

    Tshimologo Care Centre recognizes the significance of family bonds and is dedicated to facilitating Family Reunification for individuals who have experienced homelessness or separation from their families. Our team works collaboratively with relevant authorities, social workers, and support networks to help reunite individuals with their families. By providing a supportive and structured environment, we aim to assess and address the specific needs and circumstances of each individual, with the ultimate goal of reuniting them with their families.
